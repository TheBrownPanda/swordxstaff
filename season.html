How it works: At season end, levels above the season base convert to a cultivation score. Character = 100 pts/lv, Equipment = 38 pts/lv per slot, Skill = 13 pts/lv, Fantomon = 14 pts/lv, Relic = 57 pts/lv. Stars = floor(score ÷ 100) + 10 + historical. The optimizer pushes skills and fantomons first (cheapest), then calculates the equipment level needed to fill the gap — minimizing ore spend.
